Central Library

  • The college central library was established in 1994.
  • 54 national and international journals/magazines are currently available in the library.
  • The library has 4 computer terminals.
  • The college management has generously expended 60 lakhs so far on library books and journals.
  • Half-a-dozen daily newspapers are on display stands.
  • New arrival of books and magazines/journals are immediately displayed on the display racks.
  • The library collection of CDâ€™s is more than 500.
  • The department of social welfare of Government of Maharastra has sanctioned to students belonging to SC/ ST/NT category a grant of Rs. 78000 for the year 2003 and a grant of Rs. 152000 for the year 2004.
  • These grants have been gratefully utilized for 880 books for the total benefits of about 100 students intended.
  • The library facilities are availed daily on an average by 25 staff members and 150 students.
  • Almost 250 books are lent daily.
  • The students are allowed to borrow at most 2 books at a time for a week.
  • The Central library maintains separate registers for accession, reference, loan, visitors, and movement.
  • A register for fines imposed and collected is also duly maintained.
  • Almost all the Inspection Committees and other college staff on their visits to the library have complimented our college on the excellent collection of rare and many books.
  • The reference books on mathematics have always come in for very special mention.
  • I am proud to state that ours is one of the best libraries among the engineering colleges of University of Mumbai.
  • Central Library timings are 9.00 am to 8.00 pm during exam. period normally it closes at 6.00 pm.
  • The Central Library has started a separate Digital Library section for students and staff members.
  • College is subscribing a package of INDEST- AICTE consortium by spending more than Rs. 4 lakhs, to avail this facility to students and staff.
  • In this package user can access e-resources published by IEL and ASME online. It covers 120 journals, more than 95000 documents, 25000 new pages, magazines, conference proceeding and PDF images and ASME covers 19 journals published by ASME.
  • The college Central Library is determined to continue to maintain the excellent standards by implementing the following objectives in the next five years
    1. Open access library system to be introduced.
    2. Students book bank to be started.
    3. Number of books issued to students to be increased.
    4. Inclusion of more journals.
    5. Additional library staff for more efficient library service.
    6. Reprographic facility.
    7. Establishment of audio-visual room.
    8. More computer terminals to be introduced.
    9. Installation of library software and introduction of bar coding system.
    10. Membership of Delnet for inter-college library facilities in India.
    11. Internet library facility.
    12. Establishment of digital library.
    13. Total computerization of library.
  • Our college envisages the library in the next 10 years to be one of the most state-of-the-art libraries with largest collection of largest number of engineering and research titles in the book and digital form.
